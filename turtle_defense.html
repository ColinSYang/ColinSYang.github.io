Turtle Defense

Summary

A strategy game where you play as a turtle defending its nest of eggs from a variety of enemies.

Developed as the final project for Northeastern's GAME3700, Rapid Idea Prototyping.

My Contributions

  • Implemented multiple enemy types with unique AI
  • Led playtesting sessions and used the resulting data to inform changes on the current prototype

Development Information

  • Tools Used: Unity, C#
  • Duration: February 2021 - April 2021
  • Team Size: 1
  • Genre: Strategy
  • Platform: PC

What I Learned

  • How to program enemies with different behaviors in Unity
  • The importance of playtesting small changes to understand how they impact a game's experience
