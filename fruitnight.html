Fruitnight

Summary

You play as a fruit who is brand new to a kitchen. Find your smoothie partner and fight your way into the Great Blender.

Developed for Northeastern's GAME3800, Game Concept Development.

My Contributions

  • Built the framework to import branching dialogue trees from Twine to Unity using JSON serialization
  • Programmed the logic for date selection and the random pairings for the final tournament
  • Assisted with running playtests to get feedback on the game

Development Information

  • Tools Used: Unity, C#, Twine, Github
  • Duration: January 2022 - April 2022
  • Team Size: 5
  • Genre: Dating Sim / RPG
  • Platform: PC

What I Learned

  • The importance of breaking up different aspects of the game into managable tasks that can be split amongst the team
  • How to start with a core concept, polish it, and then expand on it
  • How to use events and delegates in Unity
