Noise Complaint

Summary

A chaotic action game where you attempt to ruin your noisy neigbor's party at any cost. The faster you wreck the party, the more sleep you'll be able to get.

Developed in a one week game jam as the midterm project for Northeastern's GAME3700, Rapid Idea Prototyping.

My Contributions

  • Developed a level manager script that maintained player objectives and game state
  • Architected a noise level mechanic and contributed to the overall design of the game

Development Information

  • Tools Used: Unity, C#
  • Duration: March 2021 - March 2021
  • Team Size: 4
  • Genre: Action
  • Platform: PC

What I Learned

  • The importance of being flexible and adaptable when faced with last minute errors
  • How to dynamically style text in Unity (i.e change colors, strikethrough, bold, etc.)
